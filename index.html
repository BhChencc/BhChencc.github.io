Hey there!

This is Baihui Chen. I am a PhD. student at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). I'm in the X-Group, supervised by Prof. Xueliang Li. I received my Master's degree from the Communication University of China in 2022 and was a Smart Media Lab member.

I specialize in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Human Robot Interaction (HRI), with a research focus on enhancing mental well-being through affective computing and robotic technologies. My work explores how emotionally expressive and meaningful interactions through shape-changing structures, soft robots, and remote interaction techniques. I am interested in the opportunities brought by new technologies!

Current research interests:

  • Enhancing remote interaction with human-robot interaction
  • Shape-changing structures for affective haptics or robots
