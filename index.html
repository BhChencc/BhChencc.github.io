Hey there!

[Human-Computer Interaction · Social Robot for Older Adults]

This is Baihui Chen. I am a PhD. student at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). I'm in the X-Group, supervised by Prof. Xuefang Li. I received my Master's degree from the Communication University of China in 2022 and was a Smart Media Lab member.

I focus on the fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Human Robot Interaction (HRI). Specifically, I apply social robots to improve mental health for aging and accessibility. I also used AI large language models (LLM) and embedded devices for crafting. I am interested in the opportunities brought by new technologies!

My previous research experience:

  1. Remote Computer Supported Cooperative Work,
  2. Aging and Accessibility challenges,
  3. Embodied Interaction for Children.
